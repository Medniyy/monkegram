## Reviewer notes

MonkeGram requires a compatible Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter wallet to enter.
Connecting is a sign-in action only; the app does not request a token transfer.
After entry, choose any SMB Gen2 or Gen3 by number. Grant camera and microphone
permissions, record a short clip, then verify both primary **POST TO X** and
secondary **SAVE TO DEVICE** actions. The clip is created locally and is not
uploaded to a MonkeGram backend.
